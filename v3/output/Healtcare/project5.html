dataitems

The data items and their purposes are as follows:

Patient Health Records: To provide accurate and up-to-date information for virtual consultations.

Consultation Notes: To document interactions and recommendations during virtual visits.

Prescription Data: To manage and track prescribed medications.

Appointment Schedules: To organize and manage virtual appointments.

Health Monitoring Data: To provide real-time insights into patient health status.

Insurance Information: To verify coverage for virtual consultations.

Patient Feedback: To enhance the telemedicine platform based on user input.
