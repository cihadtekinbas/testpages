The data items and their purposes are as follows:

Service Usage Data: To monitor and improve public service delivery.

Government Communications: To ensure timely communication with citizens.

Tax Records: To manage and audit tax-related data.

Employment Records: To track employment status and provide related services.

Voting Records: To maintain and secure election data.

Public Feedback: To enhance service delivery based on citizen input.
